Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Alymize collects, how we use it, and the choices you have — in plain language, no legal jargon.

1

Information We Collect

When you interact with Alymize — through our website, a strategy call, or a client project — we may collect the following:

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number, and company name when you fill out a form or book a call.
  • Business information: details about your brand, ad accounts, and marketing goals shared during onboarding.
  • Usage data: pages visited, device type, browser, and approximate location, collected automatically via analytics tools.
  • Ad platform data: performance metrics from connected accounts (Meta, Google Ads, etc.) needed to manage your campaigns.
2

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and schedule strategy calls.
  • Plan, launch, and optimize advertising campaigns on your behalf.
  • Send performance reports, invoices, and account updates.
  • Improve our website, services, and internal processes.
  • Meet legal and tax obligations where required.

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

3

Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, understand how visitors use the site, and measure the performance of our own marketing campaigns. You can disable cookies anytime through your browser settings; some site features may work differently as a result.

4

Third-Party Sharing

We work with trusted platforms and partners to deliver our services, including:

  • Advertising platforms (Meta, Google, TikTok, LinkedIn) to run and report on your campaigns.
  • Analytics providers to understand website and campaign performance.
  • Payment processors to handle invoices and billing securely.

These partners only receive the information necessary to perform their specific function and are bound by their own privacy and security obligations.

5

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures — including access controls, encrypted storage, and restricted internal access — to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, but we work continuously to keep your information safe.

6

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on where you're located, you may have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct or update inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal or contractual obligations.
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time via the unsubscribe link.

To exercise any of these rights, reach out using the contact details below.

7

Data Retention

We retain personal and campaign data for as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Once data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.
